Portugal — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists). The Polícia Judiciária (PJ) is Portugal's lead criminal investigation authority for organised crime, drugs and human trafficking. Portugal is a key Atlantic cocaine gateway for Europe and hosts MAOC-N — the EU's maritime drug interdiction centre — in Lisbon.
