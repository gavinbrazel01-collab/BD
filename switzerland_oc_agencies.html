Switzerland — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists). Switzerland has no national police force. Responsibility for policing lies with the 26 cantons. fedpol coordinates at federal level and investigates cases with international or inter-cantonal scope. A national OC strategy is being developed by end of 2026 — fedpol director Eva Wildi-Cortés has called OC her "priority", warning "the mafia is on our streets."

Analysis tools in use across agencies

Cryptocurrency tracking tools fedpol BKP used modern analytical tools to track cryptocurrency transactions as a "key" element in the 2025 Geneva watchmaking extortion case — cited in the 2025 Annual Report as a primary new investigative method
Sky ECC / Europol country packages Europol provided Switzerland a Sky ECC "country package" leading to intensive data analysis by fedpol and cantonal police forces, with first cantonal criminal court proceedings in 2023 and ongoing federal investigations
SIS II / JANUS / IPAS Switzerland participates fully in Schengen Information System (SIS II) with thousands of searches annually. JANUS is fedpol's national police information system; IPAS handles biometric data. No national i2 Analyst's Notebook confirmation — cantonal forces use varied analytical tools
