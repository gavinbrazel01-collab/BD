Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Note on Norway's architecture: Norway is the leanest system in this series — a single national police force (Politiet) with just 12 districts, two national specialist agencies (Kripos + Økokrim), and a security service (PST) that sits outside the police directorate. Kripos alone handles OC investigations, international cooperation, forensics, cybercrime and the INTERPOL NCB. The OC threat level is low but rising, with Swedish gang spillover, OMG (outlaw motorcycle gang) activity and financial fraud at record levels the dominant concerns in 2024–2025.
