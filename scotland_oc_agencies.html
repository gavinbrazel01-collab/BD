Scotland — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists). Scotland has a distinct legal system and devolved justice structure. Police Scotland's Specialist Crime Division (SCD) is the single national investigative body — there are no Scottish ROCUs. The NCA operates in Scotland but requires consent from the Lord Advocate or Scottish Ministers for certain powers.
