Italy — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Note on Italy's architecture: Italy operates a unique dual-track system — preventive (intelligence-led) and judicial (evidence-led) — running in parallel across three competing police forces (Polizia di Stato, Carabinieri, Guardia di Finanza) each with their own specialist OC units, all coordinated by a prosecutorial superstructure (DNA → DDA). No other country in this series has this level of institutional complexity.
