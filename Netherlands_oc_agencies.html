Analysis tools & investigation techniques in use

i2 Analyst's Notebook Used by the Landelijke Eenheid and regional units for link analysis, timeline charting, and network mapping of OC cases.
Financial & Digital Forensics Specialised departments within FIOD and the police for tracking financial flows and digital evidence in organised crime investigations [citation:4].
EncroChat / Sky ECC data Data from encrypted phone intercepts has been exploited by Dutch investigators, feeding into i2 for reconstructing OC networks [citation:6].
