Croatia — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's official website. Croatia occupies a critical position on the Balkan route — a primary corridor for drug trafficking, human trafficking and arms smuggling between South-East Europe and Western Europe. USKOK (specialist OC prosecution) and PNUSKOK (specialist OC police) form the twin-track system that leads all serious organised crime, corruption and trafficking cases.

web ↗ USKOK Ured za suzbijanje korupcije i organiziranog kriminaliteta — Office for the Suppression of Corruption and Organised Crime Croatia's specialist OC and anti-corruption prosecutor — formed 2001, headquartered Zagreb with nationwide jurisdiction. Prosecutes all major OC, drug trafficking, HT and corruption cases. Charges are filed with USKOK after PNUSKOK investigations complete. Sky ECC-driven drug gang prosecutions ongoing from April 2025 operation. Named a "bacro-nym" from Uskoci — Croatian guerrilla fighters against the Ottomans

Known gap: prosecutors sometimes charge traffickers with lesser offences (pandering, assault) to clear backlogs rather than full HT charges. GRETA and US TIP Report flag this consistently

Analysis tools in use across agencies

Sky ECC / Europol country package Sky ECC intercept data has been central to recent PNUSKOK/USKOK operations — the April 2025 operation arresting 45 drug-smuggling gang members was substantially built on Sky ECC evidence. USKOK charges filed directly referenced Sky ECC intercepts linking Croatian and Serbian OC figures in South American cocaine supply chains
Analytical tools (PNUSKOK / UKP) PNUSKOK and UKP use criminal intelligence analytical software for OC network mapping and financial forensics. Specific platforms are not publicly disclosed — tools are consistent with Europol-standard analytical systems. USKOK court proceedings in major OC cases reference complex analytical outputs
SIS II / Schengen / SIENA Full Schengen Information System access since January 2023. SIENA (Europol) used by PNUSKOK and UKP for operational intelligence sharing. INTERPOL NCB Zagreb manages all I-24/7 traffic. The Schengen accession significantly upgraded Croatia's access to real-time EU law enforcement intelligence
