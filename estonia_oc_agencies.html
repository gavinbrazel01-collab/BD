Estonia — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Structural note: Estonia is unusually consolidated — since 1 January 2010 the Police Board, Central Criminal Police, Border Guard and Citizenship & Migration Board have all been merged into a single agency, the Police and Border Guard Board (PPA), Estonia's largest state authority. KAPO (Internal Security Service) remains fully independent of PPA. Unlike Police, the Prosecutor's Office runs five district offices (Northern, Southern, Western, Viru, plus the Office of the Prosecutor General) while PPA itself organises into four geographic prefectures (Northern, Southern, Eastern, Western) — these two layers don't map 1:1.
