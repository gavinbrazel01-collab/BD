Latvia — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Structural note: Latvia spreads serious-crime jurisdiction across several genuinely independent bodies rather than one super-agency: the State Police (Valsts policija) for general OC and drugs, the State Security Service (VDD) for counter-intelligence-linked crime, KNAB for corruption, the State Border Guard for trafficking/smuggling at the border, and the State Revenue Service's Tax and Customs Police for financial and excise crime. Investigative competence for corruption can sit with KNAB, the Security Police, or the State Police depending on the case — there's no single "owner."
