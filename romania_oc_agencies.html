Analysis tools in use across agencies

i2 Analyst's Notebook Used by DIICOT, DCCO and key county inspectorates — entity network mapping, link analysis and timeline charting for OC, drug and HT cases; also used in JIT work with European partners
EncroChat / Sky ECC intercept data DIICOT and DCCO exploit encrypted-phone takedown data in OC network reconstruction; increasing shift to newer platforms being monitored by DCCO cybercrime investigators
ROCRIS / SIS II / Europol SIENA ROCRIS is Romania's national criminal records information system; linked to EU Schengen Information System and Europol SIENA secure channel; NCB Bucharest manages INTERPOL databases for 60,000+ officers nationally
