Greece — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Structural note: Greece's organised-crime investigation landscape changed substantially in late 2024 with the creation of the Organized Crime Division (Δ.Α.Ο.Ε. — "DAOE", dubbed the "Greek FBI" in domestic press) under Presidential Decree 29/2024. DAOE has nationwide territorial jurisdiction and reports directly to the Chief of the Hellenic Police rather than through normal regional command — all local Crime Investigation Subdivisions must now report findings up to it, a centralisation that didn't exist before October 2024. A broader Hellenic Police reorganisation under Law 7187/2025 has also restructured several directorates around it.

National investigation & intelligence agencies κεντρικές υπηρεσίες
web ↗ Δ.Α.Ο.Ε. (DAOE) Organized Crime Division (Διεύθυνση Αντιμετώπισης Οργανωμένου Εγκλήματος) Created October 2024 with nationwide jurisdiction, headquartered in Athens but commanded directly by the Chief of Hellenic Police. Five subdivisions: Narcotics; Property Crimes & Crimes Against Life; Human Trafficking & Illegal Items Trafficking; Organised Athletic Violence; and Intelligence & Operations — plus regional subdivisions for Northern Greece and Crete. Reported 139 criminal rings dismantled and €6 million seized in its first year.
