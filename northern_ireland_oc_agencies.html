Northern Ireland — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists). Northern Ireland has a uniquely complex OC landscape shaped by its paramilitary history, open land border with the Republic of Ireland, and a dual governance framework. The PSNI leads domestically; the NCA operates under Chief Constable consent; and cross-border work runs through the Joint Agency Task Force (JATF) with Irish counterparts.

Analysis tools in use across agencies

i2 Analyst's Notebook Used by PSNI OCB Intelligence Branch and NCA NI Branch — network mapping, link analysis and timeline charting of OCG and paramilitary criminal networks
POCA 2002 — Civil Recovery & Disclosure Orders NCA uniquely deploys civil recovery and tax powers under Parts 5 & 6 POCA in NI — key tool where criminal prosecution is not possible. PCTF uses Disclosure Orders as primary asset-denial tactic
ANPR / CTA monitoring / COMPASS Automatic Number Plate Recognition across NI road network; monitoring of the Common Travel Area (NI–ROI) border corridor for cash couriers; COMPASS (PSNI command/intelligence system)
