Poland — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Structural note: in late 2025 the Polish government announced a merger of CBŚP (organised crime) with CBZC (cybercrime) into a new National Investigation Bureau (Narodowe Biuro Śledcze, NBS). CBA (anti-corruption) is separately slated for closure in 2026, with powers transferring to Police. Tiles below reflect the structure as it has operated to date; flagged where the merger affects naming.
