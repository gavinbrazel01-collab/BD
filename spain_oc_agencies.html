Spain — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists). Spain has a dual-track national police system — Policía Nacional (urban) and Guardia Civil (rural, borders, coasts) — with parallel specialist OC units. Regional forces in Catalonia (Mossos d'Esquadra), the Basque Country (Ertzaintza) and Navarra (Policía Foral) also have OC jurisdiction in their territories.

Analysis tools in use across agencies

i2 Analyst's Notebook Used by UCO (Guardia Civil), UDYCO, UDEF (Policía Nacional) and Mossos d'Esquadra DIC — link analysis, network mapping and timeline charting. UCO is particularly known for sophisticated i2 use in complex OC and corruption investigations
EncroChat / Sky ECC / Ghost data Spain was one of the most active exploiters of EncroChat intercepts — hundreds of arrests. UCO and UDYCO continue processing Sky ECC and Ghost Immobiliser data for ongoing OC network prosecutions at Audiencia Nacional
SIGO / SIS II / FIES SIGO (national criminal intelligence system), EU Schengen Information System (SIS II) and FIES (high-risk prisoner file system for OC leaders in custody). SEPBLAC provides financial intelligence feeds to UDEF and UCO
