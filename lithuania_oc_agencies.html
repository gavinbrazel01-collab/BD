Lithuania — organised crime investigation agencies

Click any tile to open the agency's LinkedIn page (or official website where no LinkedIn exists).

Structural note: Lithuania's criminal police runs through a single central bureau — the Lithuanian Criminal Police Bureau (LKPB), formed in 2001 and absorbing the Police Department's Criminal Police Board in 2013 — which coordinates and controls all ten county police headquarters. Corruption (STT) and financial crime (FNTT) sit in fully separate agencies, while the Prosecutor General's Office runs its own parallel Organised Crime and Corruption Investigation (OCCI) divisions in the regional prosecutor's offices that coordinate — but don't subordinate — LKPB's and STT's casework.
